Shipping and Payment

1. How to make an order

1. How to place an order on the website

  1. Select the desired product and its quantity and click the “Add to cart” button.
  2. Go to the "Cart" section.
  3. Enter the promotional code you have and click the “Place an order” button.
  4. Enter your contact information or the recipient's contact information (full name of the recipient, phone number, email, delivery city)
  5. Select a delivery method (Courier, Order pick-up point, Pickup, Russian Post, Fast delivery from store).
  6. Select your delivery address.
  7. Choose a convenient payment method.
  8. Click the "Place an order" button.
  9. After placing an order, you will receive a message to your email address containing information about the order: order number, composition and cost of the order, and an SMS with the order number.
  10. You can always view information about your orders (current and completed) in your account in the “My Purchases” section.
  11. The maximum cost of one order is 450,000 rubles.
  12. If the cost of your order exceeds the maximum, we recommend placing several orders.

2. How to login to your account

To log into your account, you must enter your mobile phone number. Afterwards you will receive a call; to authorize, you must enter the last four digits of the incoming number.

3. How to reschedule delivery

If you want to change the delivery date or interval, please contact our Customer Support Service by phone 8 800 770 70 21 (24/7) or write to the WhatsApp or Telegram online chat, informing the operator of the order number and your first and last name.

If you want to change the recipient's phone number, contact the Customer Support Service by phone 8 800 770 70 21 (24/7) or write to the WhatsApp or Telegram online chat, informing the operator of the order number and your last name and first name.

Order status information

After successfully placing an order on the online store website, you will receive an SMS/e-mail notification of successful completion with your order number.

Order statuses can be tracked in your personal account. The Client can be notified by the Seller of an unexpected change in the status of a completed Order (delay, change in the composition of the Order, etc.) in any available way, including, but not limited to, SMS messages and messages in instant messengers (Telegram, WhatsApp, etc.).

2. Payment for the order

When placing an order in the online store, only online payment is possible. Payment on delivery is not available.

Available payment methods: bank card, gift cards, payment in shares. Bonuses may be used. When choosing the “Shares” payment method, bonuses cannot be written off.

Payment choices for a specific delivery method may vary. Read more here. Available payment methods for the Order appear after the Client selects the Order delivery method.

3. Order delivery

1. click delivery

Available payment methods (prepayment only): by bank card, gift cards, bonuses can be used.

You can place an order and call the courier when it is convenient for you to receive the parcel. We will deliver your purchases in 30–60 minutes.

How to place an order with click-to-delivery:

  • Select "click-to-delivery" at checkout.
  • We will collect the parcel, deliver it to the nearest warehouse and immediately send you a message.
  • When you are ready to receive your parcel, click on the “deliver now” button in your personal account. The courier will be with you in 30–60 minutes.
  • The shelf life of the order is 3 days. You can call the courier from 10:00 to 21:00.

If you placed an order with multiple packages, you will only be able to select click-to-delivery for one package.

Please note that click-to-click delivery is carried out in all cities where Golden Apple offline supermarkets operate. Delivery by click is available in some areas of the cities where Golden Apple supermarkets operate. You will see current delivery information when placing your order.

2. Express delivery from the store

Available payment methods (prepayment only): bank card, gift cards, bonuses can be used.

You can receive your order within 2 hours. Express delivery is valid in all cities where there are offline Golden Apple supermarkets. In St. Petersburg, Krasnodar, Khabarovsk and Sochi, delivery is carried out within 3 hours.

Delivery terms:

Express delivery cost: in Moscow and St. Petersburg - from 149 rubles, in other cities - from 99 rubles.

When ordering less than 1,500 rubles, the cost of express delivery is added to the cost of standard delivery. The cost of standard delivery is from 150 rubles.

Express delivery costs may increase during times of high demand for delivery services. The current cost is displayed when placing an order and choosing a delivery method.

If you place an order with several parcels, delivery will be free for parcels over 1,500 rubles. If the amount of parcels is less than 1,500 rubles, delivery of each of them will be paid. The cost of standard delivery is from 150 rubles. When choosing express delivery, its cost is added to the cost of standard delivery.
